Our Featured Trips

Whether you're a beginner or thrill-seeking adventurer, we have a trip designed for you.

Half Day Adventure

A perfect intro to rafting. Paddle through moderate rapids while enjoying scenic views. Ideal for families and first-timers.

Full Day Challenge

Experience class IV rapids in this adrenaline-filled full-day journey. Includes lunch and riverside rest stops.

Overnight Expedition

Camp by the riverside, raft through canyons, and bond with your crew under the stars. Includes all meals and gear.

Family Float

A calm, scenic float perfect for kids and seniors. Includes a guide who shares fun facts about the river's wildlife and history.

Sunset Rapid Run

Wrap your day with a golden-hour splash through the waves. Enjoy stunning views and moderate thrills just before sundown.

Extreme Rapids Challenge

For seasoned rafters only! Face the wildest waters and hardest drops on this expert-level ride. Safety gear provided.
